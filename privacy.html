Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

Quick Runs Cayman ("we", "us", or "our") provides concierge errands, delivery, transportation, event assistance, and government/immigration admin services across Grand Cayman. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

Who we are

Quick Runs Cayman, Cardinal Plaza, Cardinal Avenue, George Town, Grand Cayman, Cayman Islands. Contact us at info@quickruns.ky or +1 (345) 327-4460.

Information we collect

Depending on the service you request, we may collect:

  • Your name, phone number, email address, and delivery or pickup location.
  • Details about the errand, delivery, transport, or event assistance you request.
  • For admin, work permit, business licensing, and immigration services: government-issued identification, passport copies, employment or business documents, and other supporting paperwork required to prepare and submit your application.

How we use your information

  • To confirm and complete the service you requested.
  • To communicate with you about bookings, requests, and applications, usually via WhatsApp, phone, or email.
  • To prepare and submit government, immigration, or licensing paperwork on your behalf, where you have engaged us for that service.
  • To maintain records required for our business and legal obligations.

How we protect your information

We limit access to personal information, including government and immigration documents, to the team members who need it to complete your request. Documents submitted to government departments are shared only as required to complete the specific application you have asked us to handle.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with the relevant Cayman Islands government department or agency when submitting an application on your behalf, or with trusted transport and delivery partners when needed to complete a booking. We do not share government or immigration documents for any purpose beyond the service you requested.

Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the requested service, or as required by law or government record-keeping requirements.

Your rights

Under the Cayman Islands Data Protection Act, 2017, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To make a request, contact us at info@quickruns.ky.

Cookies

This website does not use advertising or tracking cookies. We may use basic technical data necessary to operate the site.

Children's privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
